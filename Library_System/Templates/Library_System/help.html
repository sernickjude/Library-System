Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center. Here you can find guidance on how to use the Library System.

📚 How to Search for Books

Use the search bar in the Catalog page to find books by title, author, or category.

📖 How to Borrow Books

Select a book and click the "Borrow" button. The book will appear in your Borrowed Books section.

🔄 How to Return Books

Go to your Borrowed Books and click "Return" once you are done reading.

⭐ How to Reserve Books

If a book is unavailable, you can reserve it and you will be notified when it becomes available.

👤 Managing Your Account

Visit the My Account page to update your profile, view history, and manage your activity.

📩 Need More Help?

If you need further assistance, please visit the Contact Us page.
