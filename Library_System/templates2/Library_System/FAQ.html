FAQ

How do I borrow a book?
Go to the Catalog page, select a book, and click the "Borrow" button.
Can I reserve books online?
Yes, unavailable books can be reserved through your account, and you'll be notified when they’re ready.
How long can I keep a book?
Books are usually borrowed for 2 weeks. Check your account for exact due dates.
